Prompt for Google Stitch

⸻

Design a premium, modern marketing website for a mobile app called Zingly (note the spelling: Zingly, not Zingli).

Brand Overview

Zingly is a property discovery platform that helps people find rooms, apartments, houses, offices, and land for rent or sale. Agents and landlords post listings, while renters and buyers browse properties and contact the agent directly.

Important: Zingly is not a booking platform and does not process payments, reservations, or contracts. The website should communicate that Zingly simply connects property seekers with property advertisers.

The goal of this website is not to explain every feature. The goal is to create a memorable first impression and encourage visitors to download the mobile app.

⸻

Design Direction

Avoid every generic startup landing page.

Do NOT create:

* Hero section with three feature cards underneath
* Generic “Why choose us”
* Stock photos of happy people
* Blue gradients
* Dashboard screenshots everywhere
* Corporate SaaS layouts

Instead, create something that feels:

* Premium
* Editorial
* Architectural
* Minimal
* Spacious
* Calm
* Human
* Modern

The website should feel closer to Apple’s product pages or a luxury architecture brand than a typical startup landing page.

⸻

Color Palette

Primary colors:

* Black (#111111)
* White (#FFFFFF)
* Off-white (#F7F7F7)

Secondary accent:

* Purple (#800080)

Purple should be used very sparingly.

Only use it for:

* Active states
* Small highlights
* Hover effects
* Selected items
* Tiny decorative accents
* Primary CTA emphasis

Never make large purple backgrounds.

The website should remain at least 90–95% black, white, and grayscale.

⸻

Overall Layout

Everything should have rounded corners.

Nothing should feel sharp.

Use large border radii across:

* Cards
* Images
* Buttons
* Containers
* Navigation
* Footer

Avoid rigid rectangular layouts.

Instead, create soft floating sections with generous spacing.

Sections should transition naturally into one another.

Avoid obvious divider lines.

Use:

* Curved edges
* Organic transitions
* Slight diagonal composition
* Soft overlapping layouts

The website should feel like one continuous experience.

⸻

Navigation

Minimal floating navigation.

Rounded container.

Contains:

* Logo
* Home
* Explore
* About
* Contact

Right side:

* Download App button

Navigation should stay elegant and minimal.

⸻

Hero Section

Large typography.

Minimal copy.

Possible headline style:

“Find somewhere worth calling home.”

Do not overload the hero with text.

The hero should include:

* Premium phone mockup
* Floating property cards around the phone
* Clean whitespace
* Download buttons

Buttons:

* Download on the App Store
* Get it on Google Play

The phone mockup should be slightly offset instead of perfectly centered.

Avoid heavy illustrations.

⸻

Property Preview Section

Instead of listing features,

show beautiful floating property preview cards.

Cards include:

* Property image
* Location
* Price
* Bedrooms
* Small “Contact Agent” indicator

Cards should overlap naturally.

No rigid grid.

⸻

Community Scrapbook (Most Important Section)

Create a section called something similar to:

“Our Visitors”

or

“The Zingly Wall”

This is an interactive marketing feature.

Visitors can type their name.

Example:

* Ralph was here
* Ama found her apartment
* Kojo visited today

After submitting,

their name appears on a large digital scrapbook.

The scrapbook should resemble handwritten notes scattered across a page.

Requirements:

* Random positioning
* Slight rotations
* Different font sizes
* Natural spacing
* Some overlap
* Organic composition

It should feel like people have signed a real notebook.

Avoid arranging names in rows or columns.

This should become one of the most memorable sections of the website.

⸻

Download Section

Keep it simple.

Large phone mockup.

Large download buttons.

Minimal copy.

Use black and white.

Purple only for small highlights.

⸻

Footer

Large rounded container.

Not a traditional rectangular footer.

Include:

* Logo
* Navigation
* Social icons
* Download buttons
* Copyright

Very clean.

⸻

Typography

Large headings.

Small supporting paragraphs.

Excellent whitespace.

Minimal copy.

The typography should carry much of the design.

⸻

Imagery

Use modern architectural photography.

High-end apartments.

Beautiful homes.

Minimal interiors.

No cheesy stock photography.

⸻

Interaction Design

Reduce major animations.

The experience should feel polished rather than flashy.

Use only subtle interactions:

* Soft hover states
* Gentle card elevation
* Smooth page scrolling
* Button press feedback
* Slight fade-in of sections
* Simple transitions between content

Avoid:

* Excessive parallax
* Large scrolling effects
* Constant floating animations
* Heavy motion graphics

The site should feel stable, premium, and refined.

⸻

UI Style

The interface should feel handcrafted.

Large spacing.

Rounded containers.

Floating cards.

Minimal shadows.

No glassmorphism.

No neumorphism.

No excessive gradients.

No visual clutter.

⸻

Tone

Visitors should immediately think:

* “This feels premium.”
* “This brand pays attention to detail.”
* “I want to explore this app.”

The website should prioritize emotion and brand identity over listing dozens of features.

Design it as a polished, premium product website that creates curiosity and encourages visitors to download the Zingly mobile app.
